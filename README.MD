## 🧠 AI Career Guide Bot

## 🚀 Project Overview
The AI Career Guide Bot is a smart assistant that helps students and young professionals find the best career paths based on their interests, skills, and education. Users can chat with the bot and answer a few questions about what they like, what they're good at, and their background. Based on this, the AI suggests career options, relevant courses, and even the top colleges offering those courses.

It uses AI to understand what the user wants and gives personalized guidance instead of general advice. It can also pull live data like salary information, job descriptions, and course details. The bot learns from documents and resources to give accurate and helpful responses, making career planning easier and more personalized gives personalized, structured guidance using OpenAI GPT and additional logic.

🎯 Features
Personalized career recommendation
Suggests relevant courses and colleges
Lists job opportunities and average salaries
Provides answers to general career queries
Uses AI reasoning with real-time data
